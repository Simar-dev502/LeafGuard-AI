About LeafGuardAI

LeafGuardAI is a cutting-edge AI-powered plant disease detection platform designed to assist farmers, gardeners, and agricultural professionals in identifying plant diseases quickly and accurately.

The system leverages modern machine learning techniques to analyze leaf images and provide instant predictions along with confidence levels, disease descriptions, and recommended treatments.

Our mission is to revolutionize agriculture by reducing crop loss, improving productivity, and making advanced plant diagnostics accessible to everyone.

With a user-friendly interface and real-time analysis, LeafGuardAI bridges the gap between technology and farming, empowering users to make smarter decisions.
